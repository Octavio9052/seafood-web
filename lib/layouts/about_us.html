About us

Established in early 2017, River Salmon is currently one of the top seafood restaurant in north america. It all started back in the small port city of Ensenada, Mexico. Even then, the high quality of our dishes and our modern approach has been our unique signature that makes us different than the rest of the market. We enjoy bringing the best the sea has to offer to your food. Our mission is to always keep the client satisfied and with a smile in the face.
